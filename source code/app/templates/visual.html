Worldwide Terrorims and Public Attention

Terrorist Attacks We Paid Attention To

The problem of terrorism is most severe for Western Asia countries, such as Iraq, Pakistan, and Afghanistan, each having more than 10k terrorist attacks in the last 45 years. The Western Asia also has the highest public attention, measured by the number of Google searches on corresponding attacks.

The Trend

Evolution of Terrorism and Public Attention in the Last 45 Years

The fatalities and injuries of terrorist attacks have increased steadily over the past 45 years. Those numbers have a peak at the time of 9/11. A steep rise has also happened since 2011. On the other hand, public attention has been stable and even decreased gradually.

The Weapons

Commonly Used Weapons

Explosives and bombs are the most commonly used weapons and have caused the highest death toll.

The Attacks

Different Types of Attacks

An attack is defined as international if the nationality of the perpetrator group is different from the location of the attack. Around 90% of attacks are international. An attack is considered unsuccessful depending on different tactics. For example, if a bomb is planted and fails to detonate or an assasin attempts and fails to kill the intended target, then an attack is not successful. About 90% of attacks are successful and more than 95% are suicide attacks.

The Victims

Most Common Victim Types

Private citizens and properties are the most common victims of terrorist attacks. Within private citizens and properties, unnamed civilians make the highest percentage of both fatalities and injuries. This pattern may vary across countries. For example, business and goverment have had attacked the most and the police has the highest number of deaths, and injuries in the US.
